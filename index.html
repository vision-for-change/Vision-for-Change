The Problem

Why Eye Care Access Matters

Millions are left behind simply because of where they were born or how much money they have. This is a solvable crisis.

1
🎒
Education Impact
Children with uncorrected vision struggle in school. Studies show vision problems are behind millions of misdiagnosed learning disabilities globally.
2
💼
Economic Impact
The global productivity loss from unaddressed vision impairment is estimated at over $400 billion annually — robbing people of livelihoods and opportunities.
3
🏥
Access Gap
In low-income regions, one optometrist may serve over 100,000 people. Cost, distance, and awareness are the primary barriers to care.
Protect Your Vision

Simple Steps to Healthier Eyes

🖥️
Screen Breaks
Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.
🥕
Nutrition
Foods rich in Vitamins A, C, E, and omega-3 fatty acids help maintain good vision and reduce the risk of age-related conditions.
👁️
Regular Checkups
Routine eye exams catch problems early. Many conditions like glaucoma show no symptoms until significant damage has occurred.
🕶️
Sun Protection
UV rays damage the cornea and lens over time. Wear sunglasses with 100% UV protection and wide-brimmed hats outdoors.
