The Crisis

An Invisible Barrier to Human Potential

Vision loss is not just a health issue; it's an economic and educational crisis. Without clear sight, children can't learn, adults can't work, and communities can't thrive.

Education

Children with vision impairment are up to 5x less likely to complete their education.

Economy

Global productivity loss due to vision impairment exceeds $411 Billion annually.

1 in 7

People globally live with vision loss because they lack access to basic eye care.

Our Strategy

Three Pillars of Direct Action

We tackle the eye care gap through a holistic approach that combines immediate assistance with long-term systemic change.

Demystifying eye health and providing communities with the knowledge to prevent vision loss through early detection and proper hygiene. We conduct workshops and distribute educational materials globally.
Directly funding eye exams and corrective lenses for marginalized populations. By partnering with local clinics, we ensure that financial constraints never stand in the way of a child's ability to see and learn.
Mobilizing the next generation to advocate for policy changes. We work with governments to integrate eye care into universal health coverage, creating sustainable, long-term impact for millions.
