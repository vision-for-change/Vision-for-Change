Eye Health

Simple Habits.
Lasting Sight.

WHO Resources
01
🖥️
Screen Breaks

The 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. It dramatically reduces digital eye strain and fatigue.

↗
02
🥕
Nutrition

Vitamins A, C, E, zinc, and omega-3s protect against macular degeneration and cataracts. Eat leafy greens, fish, and colourful vegetables.

↗
03
👁️
Regular Checkups

Conditions like glaucoma have no symptoms until significant damage occurs. Annual exams catch problems early when they're most treatable.

↗
04
🕶️
Sun Protection

UV exposure accumulates over a lifetime. Wear sunglasses with 100% UVA/UVB protection outdoors to prevent cataracts and long-term damage.
