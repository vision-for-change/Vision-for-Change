👁️ Vision for Change

Eyes matter. Access matters.

We are a youth-led initiative committed to making eye care accessible and affordable for everyone. Vision is a vital part of daily life, yet millions of people worldwide struggle to get the care they need. Our goal is to raise awareness about eye health, highlight the costs that prevent people from seeking care, and inspire our community to take action. Together, we can ensure that everyone has the opportunity to see clearly.

1B+

People globally need vision correction but lack access.

80%

Impact

Uncorrected vision problems affect education, work, and quality of life.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision for Change is founded and run by youth who are passionate about eye health. We believe that everyone deserves access to affordable eye care, and education is the first step.

Our Mission:

  • Educate communities about the importance of eye health and prevention of vision problems.
  • Reduce barriers to eye care by spreading awareness about costs and resources.
  • Inspire action through campaigns, advocacy, and fundraising for affordable vision solutions.

We envision a world where clear vision is a right, not a privilege, and we are taking the first steps to make that a reality in our communities.

Protect Your Vision

Screen Breaks

Follow the 20-20-20 rule—every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

Nutrition

Eat foods rich in vitamins A, C, and E to support eye health.

Regular Checkups

Eye exams can detect issues early and prevent long-term problems.

Sun Protection

Wear sunglasses with UV protection to protect your eyes from harmful rays.
